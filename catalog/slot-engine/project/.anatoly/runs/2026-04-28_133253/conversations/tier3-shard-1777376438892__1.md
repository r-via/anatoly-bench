### Finding 2: `weightedPick` (rng.ts:5)

**Duplication confirmed** — `pickFromWeighted` in `reels.ts:30-41` is algorithmically identical to `weightedPick` in `rng.ts:5-16`:

**Verdict:** NEEDS_FIX confirmed at similar confidence.
